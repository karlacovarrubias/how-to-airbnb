Host Wisdom

4 Rules of Great Hosting

01
First Impression = Listing Photos
Style your space before shooting. Natural light + neutral tones = more bookings. Hire a photographer once if possible.
02
Comfort Over Decoration
A good mattress beats a designer lamp every time. Prioritize sleep quality, hot water, and fast WiFi above all else.
03
Make Check-In Frictionless
Self-check-in with a smart lock, clear instructions, and a welcome message is the single best upgrade you can make.
04
Stock for a Week-Long Stay
Extra towels, toilet paper, coffee, cooking oil. Guests on longer stays run out of basics — anticipate their needs.
